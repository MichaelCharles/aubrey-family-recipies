# Ingredients

- 1 cup sour cream
- 1 can (16 oz.) creamed corn
- 1 can (16 oz.) whole kernel corn, drained
- 1 box (8.5 oz.) Jiffy Corn Muffin Mix
- 1/4 cup butter, melted

# Directions
